# Transitive relation
A [[binary relation|relation]] $R$ on a [[set]] $S$ is called **transitive** if whenever $(a, b)\in R$ and $(b, c) \in R$, then $(a, c)\in R$, for all $a, b, c \in A$. 

> $$\forall x\, \forall y\, \forall z\, [(x, y)\in R\land (y, z)\in R\rightarrow (x,z)\in R]$$
