# Onto
See [[surjective]] for more information.

A [[First Year/function]] is **onto** if its [[range]] is equal to its [[codomain]].

A [[linear transformation]] $T$ maps $\mathbb{R}^n$ **onto** $\mathbb{R}^m$ iff the columns of $A$ ([[standard matrix]]) [[span]] $\mathbb{R}^m$.
