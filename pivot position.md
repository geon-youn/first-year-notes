# Pivot Positions
A **pivot position** is a location that corresponds to a leading 1 in the [[reduced row echelon form|RREF]]. 

```ad-example
$$\begin{bmatrix}
\textbf{0} & 1 & -13\\
1 & \textbf{4} & 21\\
0 & 0 & 0\end{bmatrix}\Leftrightarrow\begin{bmatrix}\textbf{1} & 4 & 21\\
0 & \textbf{1} & -13\\
0 & 0 & 0\end{bmatrix}$$
```
