# Nullity
The **nullity** of a matrix $A$ is the [[dimension]] of $A$'s [[null space]].

```ad-info
title: Nomenclature
$\text{nullity}A\equiv \text{dim Nul}A$
```

```ad-tip
title: Alternative explanation
Nullity = the number of non-[[pivot column|pivot columns]] in $A$
```
