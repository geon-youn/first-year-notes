# Maximum and Minimum
Let $A\subseteq\mathbb{R}$. 

$M$ is a **maximum element** of $A$ if $M\in A$ and $M$ is an [[bound|upper bound]] of $A$. 

$m$ is a **minimum element** of $A$ if $m\in A$ and $m$ is a [[bound|lower bound]] of $A$. 

Max and min of a set are unique.
