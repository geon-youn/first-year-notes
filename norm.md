# Length (Norm)
The length (or norm) of a vector $\vec{v}$ is the nonnegative scalar $||\vec{v}||$ defined as the square root of its [[inner product]] such that
$$||\vec{v}||=\sqrt{\vec{v}\cdot\vec{v}}=\sqrt{v_1^2+\dots+v_n^2}$$
equivalently, 
$$||\vec{v}||^2=\vec{v}\cdot\vec{v}$$
