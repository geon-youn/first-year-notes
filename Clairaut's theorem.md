# Clairaut's theorem
Suppose a [[functions of two variables]] $f$ is defined on a disk $D$ that contains the point $(a, b)$. If the [[second partial derivative]]s $f_{xy}$ and $f_{yx}$ are both continuous on $D$, then 

> $$f_{xy}(a,b)=f_{yx}(a,b)$$
